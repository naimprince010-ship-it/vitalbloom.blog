# AdSense Publishing Checklist

Use this checklist before applying for Google AdSense.

## Required Site Pages

- About
- Contact
- Privacy Policy
- Terms
- Disclaimer
- Cookie Policy

## Content Quality Checks

Each article should have:

- Original writing, not copied from another site.
- A clear reader problem and a useful answer.
- 900-1,500 words where the topic needs depth.
- Short paragraphs and scannable headings.
- No exaggerated medical claims.
- A health disclaimer where medical or wellness advice appears.
- A meta title and meta description in Yoast.
- One featured image.
- At least 2 internal links to related VitalBloom articles.
- At least one FAQ section where it helps the reader.

## Technical Checks

- Submit sitemap in Google Search Console.
- Confirm `https://www.vitalbloom.blog` is the preferred domain.
- Check that `https://vitalbloom.blog` redirects to `https://www.vitalbloom.blog`.
- Confirm `https://backend.vitalbloom.blog/graphql` works.
- Fix broken links before applying.
- Make sure mobile layout is clean.
- Avoid empty categories and placeholder content.

## Recommended Before Applying

- Publish at least 25-30 complete posts.
- Wait until Google indexes the main pages and some articles.
- Keep navigation simple and visible.
- Remove placeholder text that looks unfinished.
- Do not overload pages with ads before approval.

## Article Template

Suggested article structure:

1. Intro: explain the reader problem clearly.
2. Main answer: give practical advice first.
3. Detailed sections: explain habits, examples, and mistakes to avoid.
4. Safety note: use careful language for health topics.
5. FAQ: answer 3-5 related questions.
6. Conclusion: summarize the next realistic step.

## Health Disclaimer Text

Use this where appropriate:

This article is for general informational purposes only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always speak with a qualified healthcare professional about personal health concerns.
