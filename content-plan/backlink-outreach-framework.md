# VitalBloom Backlink Outreach Framework

Goal: earn relevant editorial links from trustworthy wellness, health education, workplace wellness, and lifestyle sites without spam tactics.

## Target Types

- Small clinics with patient resource pages
- Dietitian, therapist, fitness coach, and wellness practitioner blogs
- University or community wellness newsletters
- Remote-work and HR wellness blogs
- Local health nonprofits and community resource pages
- Podcast/newsletter roundups that cite practical wellness tools

## Qualification Checklist

- The site has real editorial content and visible ownership.
- The page topic matches sleep, nutrition, fitness, mindfulness, or workplace wellness.
- The site links out to helpful resources naturally.
- The content is not a link farm, coupon page, or unrelated directory.
- The outreach angle is specific to one VitalBloom asset.

## Outreach Angles

- Sleep: offer the printable sleep hygiene checklist as a simple reader handout.
- Nutrition: offer the balanced plate guide as a non-diet meal planning resource.
- Remote work: offer the remote worker wellness checklist for HR and productivity audiences.
- Beginner wellness: offer the 7-day routine for readers who need a low-pressure starting point.

## Email Template

Subject: Useful wellness checklist for your readers

Hi [Name],

I found your resource on [topic/page] and noticed you share practical wellness guidance for [audience].

We published a concise [asset name] that may fit your readers because it gives [specific benefit] without complicated rules or medical claims:

[URL]

It may be a useful addition near your section on [specific section/topic]. Either way, I appreciate the work you are doing to make wellness advice more practical.

Best,
VitalBloom Editorial Team

## Weekly Outreach Rhythm

- Build 25 qualified targets.
- Send 10 personalized emails.
- Follow up once after 7-10 days.
- Track responses, links earned, and topics that get interest.
- Use replies to improve the next asset.
