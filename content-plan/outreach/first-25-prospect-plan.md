# First 25 Backlink Prospect Plan

Goal: build a small, qualified outreach list before sending any emails. The first 25 targets should be relevant, manually reviewed, and mapped to one asset each.

## Target Mix

- 8 sleep/wellness resource pages for `sleep-hygiene-checklist-printable`
- 8 nutrition/meal-planning resource pages for `balanced-plate-printable-guide`
- 7 remote work/workplace wellness pages for `remote-worker-wellness-checklist`
- 2 flexible targets based on whichever asset has the strongest fit

## Qualification Workflow

1. Search using the queries in `backlink-prospecting-queries.md`.
2. Open the page and confirm it has a real audience fit.
3. Check whether the page already links to outside resources.
4. Identify the exact section where the VitalBloom asset could fit.
5. Find a contact email or contact form.
6. Add the prospect to `vitalbloom-backlink-target-tracker.csv`.
7. Write a one-sentence personalization note before sending.

## Scoring

Score each prospect from 1 to 5:

- 5: highly relevant resource page, clearly links to outside resources, easy contact path.
- 4: relevant blog/resource page with visible ownership and a decent placement fit.
- 3: relevant site, but placement or contact path is less clear.
- 2: weak fit, likely not worth first-round outreach.
- 1: avoid.

Only contact prospects scored 4 or 5 in the first outreach round.

## First Outreach Batch

Send 10 emails first:

- 4 sleep asset pitches
- 3 nutrition asset pitches
- 3 remote work asset pitches

Wait 7-10 days, then send one follow-up. Track every contact attempt in the CSV.

## Success Metrics

- 25 qualified prospects collected
- 10 first-round emails sent
- 5 follow-ups sent
- 1-3 link placements or positive replies
- Notes collected on which asset gets the best response

## Next Assets to Build After Outreach Starts

- 7-Day Beginner Wellness Routine
- Beginner Home Workout Checklist
- Stress Reset Worksheet

Build the next asset based on which topic gets the strongest outreach response.
