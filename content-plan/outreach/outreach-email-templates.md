# VitalBloom Outreach Email Templates

Keep outreach concise and specific. Personalize the first line and the placement suggestion.

## Brand Anchor Rules

Use one of these branded anchors when asking for a general site mention or when a resource page allows descriptive anchor text:

- VitalBloom Blog
- VitalBloom.blog
- VitalBloom wellness guides
- VitalBloom Blog wellness resources

Avoid asking partners to link with generic anchors such as "click here" or with medical claims. The goal is to help Google associate the official wellness publication with `https://www.vitalbloom.blog/`.

## Social Profile Setup Checklist

Create the same profile identity on each platform before heavy outreach:

- Profile name: VitalBloom Blog
- Website: https://www.vitalbloom.blog/
- Bio: Evidence-informed wellness guides for nutrition, fitness, sleep, stress, mindfulness, and healthy living.
- Contact: hello@vitalbloom.blog
- First links to include: About, Why Trust VitalBloom, Editorial Policy, and the four pillar guides.

Recommended profiles: Pinterest, Facebook Page, LinkedIn Page, and X/Twitter. Use only profiles that can be maintained; do not list inactive social links on the site until they exist.
