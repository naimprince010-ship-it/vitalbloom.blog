# VitalBloom Backlink Prospecting Queries

Use these search queries to build a qualified list before outreach. Prioritize pages that already link to helpful resources and have a real audience fit.

## Sleep Hygiene Checklist Printable

- `sleep hygiene resources wellness blog`
- `sleep hygiene checklist resource page`
- `site:.edu sleep hygiene resources students`
- `site:.org sleep hygiene tips resources`
- `clinic sleep hygiene handout resources`
- `wellness newsletter sleep hygiene checklist`

Best fit:

- student wellness pages
- clinic resource libraries
- therapist or counselor blogs
- workplace wellness newsletters
- sleep education roundups

Pitch angle:

Use VitalBloom's printable-style checklist as a practical weekly sleep reset for readers who need a simple routine, not a medical treatment plan.

## Remote Worker Wellness Checklist

- `remote worker wellness checklist`
- `remote work wellbeing resources`
- `work from home wellness resources`
- `HR remote employee wellness checklist`
- `remote work ergonomics breaks resources`
- `coworking remote worker wellness blog`

Best fit:

- HR and employee wellness blogs
- remote work newsletters
- coworking resource pages
- productivity blogs that discuss breaks
- small business wellness pages

Pitch angle:

Use VitalBloom's remote worker checklist as a practical handout for movement breaks, eye rest, lunch boundaries, hydration, and shutdown routines.

## Qualification Rules

- The page should be relevant to the asset.
- The site should have real editorial content and visible ownership.
- Avoid link farms, coupon directories, AI spam pages, and unrelated guest post farms.
- Prefer pages that already link to outside resources.
- Choose one asset per outreach email.
