# VitalBloom Next 15 Content Plan

Goal: publish safer long-tail wellness content for USA/Canada readers while AdSense review is pending. These topics avoid diagnosis/treatment claims and focus on practical routines, habits, nutrition basics, sleep, movement, and stress management.

## Publishing Rules

- Target length: 900-1,200 words each.
- Include: clear intro, 5-7 H2 sections, checklist, Common Questions, 2-4 credible references.
- Use cautious language: "may help", "can support", "for general education".
- Avoid: disease treatment promises, supplement claims, extreme weight-loss claims, copied medical advice.
- Internal links: add 3-5 links to existing posts per article.
